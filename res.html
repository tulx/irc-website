IRC 2018 was held in Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) from 6th to 8th January, 2018. The winner of IRC 2018 was team Mars Rover Manipal from Manipal Institute of Technology, India, followed by team LU_Durbar from Bangladesh and team Bracu Mongol Tori from BRAC University, Bangladesh as first and second runners-up respectively.
